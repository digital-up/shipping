Ship Chartering

Our company is engaged in offering and providing worldwide shipbroker and chartering services of all kinds of oil and oil product cargoes, project cargoes, breakbulk cargo, and dry bulk cargo such as minerals, logs, timber, steels, fertilizers, coal, and grains in a completely professional manner.

A highly motivated team of chartering, post-fixing, operations managers in full cooperation with shipbrokers, who are providing reliable, efficient, and costs effective services to our valuable clients, managing all aspects of shipping, including mainly chartering.

We have in-depth knowledge and high industry experience in providing charter services. This service is extremely demanded transporting of various cargoes including oil products as well as bulk, dry cargoes to various destinations across the globe. Our personnel are experienced in the transportation/logistics of project cargoes, oil and oil products, bulk grain trades, dangerous bulk cargoes, steel rails, minerals, etc.

The company believes that providing customers with good and timely performance ensures their satisfaction and in turn affords us further opportunities to develop our business and strive for further prosperity.
Our offered services include:

• The brokerage of cargoes on an exclusive or non-exclusive basis
• The operation of ocean going and coastal vessels on time charter or voyage basis
• Commercial ship management
• Up to date advice on all required documentation and declarations
